THE #HANDSOFF CAMPAIGN

Young people can contact childline for support on
0800 1111 or www.childline.org

Adults worried about a child can contact the NSPCC Helpline on
0808 800 5000 or www.nspcc.org.uk

If you or someone you know is experiencing domestic abuse
contact Women's Aid www.womensaid.org.uk

For specialist, confidential services for sexual violence survivors.
Contact the national Rape Crisis helpline on
0808 802 9999 or rapecrisis.org.uk
